How to Unlock Normal Raids Roulette in FFXIV: A Guide for Beginners

Final Fantasy XIV (FFXIV) offers a wide range of exciting and challenging content, including raids. Raids are group-based battles that require coordination, strategy, and skill to successfully complete. These challenging battles often reward players with valuable loot and gear upgrades. The Normal Raids Roulette is one of the best ways to experience all the regular raids available in the game while offering a great opportunity to earn additional rewards. Step 1: Reach Level 50

To unlock the Normal Raids Roulette, you need to be at least level 50. However, before you jump into high-level content, you must follow the main storyline quests and reach this milestone level by completing these tasks. Once you reach level 50, you will need to complete the initial raid storyline quests. These quests include “The Binding Coil of Bahamut,” “The Second Coil of Bahamut,” and “The Final Coil of Bahamut” campaigns. After completing the initial questline, you will also need to complete the “Heavensward” and “Stormblood” expansions, which will grant you access to the Alexander and Omega Raids, respectively. Step 5: Unlock Normal Raids Roulette
